Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, peppercorns, and bay leaves.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the marinated chicken until brown.
  4. Pour in the marinade and water. Simmer until the chicken is cooked and the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your Chicken Adobo!
