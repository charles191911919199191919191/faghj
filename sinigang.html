Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork ribs or pork belly, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 large radish, sliced
  • 1 large eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 liters water
  • 1 packet sinigang mix
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add pork and simmer until meat is tender.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in sinigang mix and add radish, eggplant, and string beans. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy your Sinigang!
