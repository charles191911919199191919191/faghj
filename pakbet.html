Pakbet

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup squash, sliced
  • 1 cup okra
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 medium-sized bitter gourd (ampalaya), sliced
  • 1 medium-sized eggplant, sliced
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 1/4 cup pork belly, sliced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© onion and tomato until soft.
  2. Add pork belly (if using) and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in shrimp paste and add the rest of the vegetables. Cook until vegetables are tender but not overcooked.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy your Pakbet!
