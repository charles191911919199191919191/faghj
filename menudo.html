Menudo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup pork liver, sliced
  • 1 large potato, diced
  • 1 large carrot, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion in a large pot until fragrant.
  2. Add pork and cook until browned. Add pork liver and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Add bay leaves and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell pepper. Continue simmering until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy your Menudo!
